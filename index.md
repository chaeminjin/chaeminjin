# Minjin Chae

PhD Candidate, Harvard University Department of Sociology

email: **minjinchae@g.harvard.edu**

I study how individual decisions and behaviors regarding work and family, often perceived as voluntary and rational, are in fact deeply embedded in social structures and norms. My projects ask whether providing workplace flexibility shifts gendered expectations for domestic labor, how occupational segregation produces gender and racial inequality in remote work, and how organizational configurations mediate the effects of expansions in workplace flexibility and public childcare. I use quantitative methods with administrative, survey, and experimental data from the U.S., Germany, and South Korea.
